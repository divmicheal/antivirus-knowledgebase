How to Log Into Your Bitdefender Account
========================================

Logging into your Bitdefender account allows you to manage subscriptions, devices, and security settings.

Steps to Access Account
-----------------------

• Visit the official login page  
• Enter your registered email address  
• Enter your secure password  
• Click Sign In  

If two-factor authentication is enabled, enter the verification code sent to your email or mobile device.

Common Login Issues
-------------------

• Incorrect password  
• Expired session  
• Browser cache issues  
• Account locked due to multiple attempts  

If login fails, reset your password using the recovery option.
